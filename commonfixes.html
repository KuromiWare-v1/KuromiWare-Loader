# Cant login

-------------------------------------------------

go to control panel (windows)

then proceed to network and internet

then network and sharing center

then change adapter settings

then choose what ever you use | Ethernet or Wi-Fi |

right click either one and choose properties

then click internet protocal version 4 (tcp/ipv4)

then click properties and choose "Use the following dns server adresses"

change preferred to: 1.1.1.1
change alternate to: 1.0.0.1

then use a vpn
